The Generative Ecosystem - HOW IT WORKS!

The project is built on some seriously cool ideas: a smart computer simulation, a way for sounds to be chosen, and even a nature-inspired sound organizer! It's like a digital terrarium where cool soundscapes just... happen!


1. Swarmscape: Boids Algorithm

The system's little critters (we call 'em "boids") follow three simple rules, like birds in a flock. It's all about emergent behavior - that means complex stuff from simple rules!

  • Separation: Don't bump into your buddies!
  • Alignment: Try to go the same way as nearby buddies.
  • Cohesion: Stick close to the center of your group.

2. Auspex: Sound Choices

The "Auspex" part lets boids pick sounds. It uses a tool called FluCoMa to map out a huge sound library by their "timbre" (fancy word for sound character). Boids then cruise this "sound map" and pick tunes based on how they're feeling or moving!

Example: Fast boids might pick sharp sounds, calm boids pick smooth sounds.

3. Acoustic Niche Theory

To stop it sounding like a mess, boids find their own "sonic space" or frequency, like animals in a jungle. This keeps the soundscape clear and interesting!
